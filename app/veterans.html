Veterans Program

Honoring Those Who Serve

Nominate a Veteran to Ride in the Parade

Krewe Mystique de la Capitale is proud to honor our nation's veterans. Our special Veterans Float program provides a unique celebration and parade experience exclusively for non-member veterans and service members.

Each year, we invite veterans to ride in our parade, participate in our celebration, and receive throws and recognition for their service. This is our way of saluting those who have dedicated themselves to our country.

Program Details

  • Free participation for eligible veterans
  • Dedicated float experience
  • Special throws and recognition
  • Community celebration

How to Nominate

To nominate a veteran or to learn more about our Veterans Float program, please contact us with the following information:

  • Veteran's full name
  • Service branch and years of service
  • Contact information
  • Any special circumstances or preferences

Krewe Mystique is honored to serve those who have served our country. Your participation helps us celebrate and recognize the sacrifices made by our veterans.
