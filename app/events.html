Community Celebrations & Member Traditions

It's a Celebration

At the core of Krewe Mystique de la Capitale is a special bond established between our members. This foundation develops into friendships that last a lifetime, creating a community bound by shared passion for tradition and celebration.

Our events bring together members, families, and community supporters to celebrate Mardi Gras culture, maintain traditions, and strengthen the bonds that make Krewe Mystique unique.

King Cake Party

An annual tradition that members look forward to each year. The executive members of Krewe Mystique put on their chef coats and throw a huge party with delicious food and what is widely recognized as the best King Cake in the city.

This celebration brings together the krewe family for good food, good company, and unforgettable memories.

Parade Season Events

Throughout the parade season, Krewe Mystique hosts events for members, invited guests, and community participants. From pre-parade gatherings to post-parade celebrations, there are opportunities to engage with our krewe community.
